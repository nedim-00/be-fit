About us

We are in Sarajevo, in shopping center "Alta"

Modernly equipped fitness center

BeFit is an exciting, brand new fitness destination in Sarajevo with over 500 square metres of space in the Alta Shopping Center. Top equipment from renowned world manufacturers Matrix and Technogym, will provide you with a unique experience of fitness.

Group trainings

BeFit offers various types of training for different condition levels of participants. Our goal is to offer a selection of group classes that suits your needs and fast pace of life. Professional and experienced trainers will help you achieve the desired results. Try our group workouts and have fun feeling healthier and happier!

Private trainings

Achieve your fitness goals with one-on-one personal training tailored to your needs or gather a group of 3-5 friends who will work together with a personal trainer.
